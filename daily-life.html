🌅 A day at Sunshine Ridge

A warm, consistent routine

Predictable rhythms help children feel secure and ready to learn. Here's what a typical day looks like — from morning drop-off to afternoon pickup.

What a day looks like

Our daily rhythm

This is a typical preschool room schedule. Infant and toddler rooms follow the same general arc, adapted to younger children's needs.

  1. 🌅

    6:30 – 8:30 am

    Morning Arrival & Free Play

    Children arrive and ease into the day with open play, puzzles, and books at their own pace.

  2. 🧩

    8:30 – 10:00 am

    Morning Snack & Circle Time

    Breakfast snack, followed by group calendar, weather, and story time together on the rug.

  3. 🌿

    10:00 – 11:15 am

    Learning Centers & Work Time

    Children rotate through Montessori-inspired centers — art, math, reading, science, and practical life.

    Montessori-Inspired
  4. 🛝

    11:15 am – 12:00 pm

    Outdoor Playtime

    Gross motor play, nature exploration, and fresh air on our fully fenced playground.

  5. 🍽️

    12:00 – 1:00 pm

    Lunch

    Hot, nutritious lunch served family-style. Children help set and clear the table — a Montessori practical life moment.

    Montessori-Inspired
  6. 😴

    1:00 – 2:30 pm

    Rest Time

    Nap or quiet rest on cots. Older children who don't sleep enjoy quiet books or drawing.

  7. 🎨

    2:30 – 6:00 pm

    Afternoon Activities & Pickup

    Afternoon snack, a rotating enrichment activity (art, music, science, cooking), outdoor play, and gradual pickup through 6 pm.
