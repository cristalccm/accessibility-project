Hello, we're Sugar Petal Bakery

Started in a tiny farmhouse kitchen in 2012, Sugar Petal grew from a weekend farmers' market booth into your neighborhood's favorite bakery.

We believe baked goods should taste like someone made them just for you — because someone did. Everything is hand-shaped, slow-fermented, and finished by our small team of passionate bakers.

  • Locally sourced dairy and seasonal fruit
  • No artificial additives or preservatives
  • Custom cake & event orders welcome
  • Vegan and gluten-friendly options available
