Got questions?

Parents ask us all the time

For infants: labeled formula or breast milk, diapers, wipes, 2–3 changes of clothing, and a favorite comfort item. For toddlers and preschoolers: a labeled backpack, 2 changes of clothing, and a water bottle. We provide everything else — meals, sunscreen, art supplies. A full supply list is sent after enrollment.
Yes! We are an approved Arkansas Child Care Assistance Program provider. We also accept employer-sponsored childcare benefits and FSA/dependent care accounts. Contact us to walk through which financial assistance options may apply to your family.
Yes. We hold a current Arkansas Department of Human Services childcare license and receive regular unannounced inspections. We maintain a 5-star rating through the Arkansas Better Beginnings quality rating system. Inspection reports are publicly available and posted in our lobby.
We'll call you immediately if your child develops a fever over 100.4°F, vomits, or shows signs of illness that may affect other children. A parent or authorized pickup must arrive within one hour. Children may return after being symptom-free for 24 hours without medication. Our sick-child policy is provided in full at enrollment.

Infant spots fill quickly — often 3–6 months in advance. Toddler and preschool spots are more available, especially mid-year.
